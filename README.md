# 📊 HR Analytics – Workforce, Attrition & Risk Analysis (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an **end-to-end HR Analytics solution** built to analyze workforce composition, attrition trends, and employee risk factors.  
The goal is to help HR leadership **identify high-risk employees**, understand **attrition drivers**, and take **data-driven HR actions** using interactive Power BI dashboards.

The solution covers the complete analytics lifecycle — from **raw data ingestion and SQL transformation** to **Power BI modeling, DAX calculations, and business-ready insights**.


## 🎯 Business Problems Addressed

- Limited visibility into workforce size and composition  
- No clear understanding of attrition drivers  
- Difficulty identifying high-risk employees early  
- Promotion delays and overtime risks not tracked effectively  
- HR decisions not supported by centralized, data-backed insights  

---

## 🧠 Project Objectives
- Build a centralized HR analytics system  
- Create multi-page dashboards for major HR functions  
- Identify attrition drivers and high-risk employee segments  
- Enable proactive, data-driven HR decision-making 

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

- **SQL (MySQL)** – Data cleaning, transformation, joins  
- **Excel** – Initial data validation  
- **Power Query** – ETL and data preparation  
- **DAX** – KPI, attrition, and risk calculations  
- **Power BI** – Interactive dashboards and storytelling 

---

## 🔄 Data Pipeline & Workflow
1. Raw employee CSV data loaded into MySQL  
2. Data cleaned, standardized, and validated using SQL  
3. Employee and attrition data joined into an analysis-ready dataset  
4. Cleaned data imported into Power BI  
5. Power Query used for transformations and feature engineering  
6. DAX measures created for KPIs, attrition metrics, and risk indicators  
7. Insights delivered through multi-page Power BI dashboards  

---

## 📂 Dataset Summary
- **1,470 employee records**  
- **35 HR attributes per employee**  
- Covers demographics, job details, tenure, performance, satisfaction, and attrition indicators  

---

## 📊 Dashboards Included

### 1️⃣ Executive Overview
- High-level workforce KPIs for leadership decision-making  
- Workforce size, gender split, tenure, and job level distribution  

### 2️⃣ Workforce Analysis
- Department and role-wise employee distribution  
- Experience, job level mix, satisfaction, and overtime patterns  

### 3️⃣ Attrition Analysis
- Department-wise attrition and retrenchment  
- Promotion delays, attrition reasons, and satisfaction risk  

### 4️⃣ Drivers & Risk Analysis
- Identification of key attrition drivers  
- High-risk employee segments by role, tenure, and department  

### 5️⃣ Actionable Insights & HR Interventions
- Prioritized list of high-risk employees  
- Promotion, workload balancing, and retention recommendations  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- **1,470 employees** analyzed across the organization  
- **Sales and R&D** show the highest attrition risk  
- **72 employees** overdue for promotion  
- **Overtime and low satisfaction** are top attrition drivers  
- **86 employees** identified as high-risk  

---

## 💼 Business Impact
- Enables early identification of attrition risk  
- Supports promotion planning and workload balancing  
- Helps reduce overtime and employee burnout  
- Improves retention through targeted HR interventions  
- Strengthens workforce stability and strategic HR planning  

---

## 🔑 Key Recommendations
- Fast-track promotions for employees with **4+ years in the same role**  
- Reduce overtime in **Sales, R&D, and IT** through workload rebalancing  
- Prioritize **high-risk employees** with targeted retention interventions  
- Strengthen engagement programs for **mid-tenure (2–4 years)** employees  
- Apply **role-specific retention strategies** for high-attrition roles  
- Monitor attrition risk **monthly using KPI dashboards**
